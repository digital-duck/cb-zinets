🌸 Phrase 众所周知

Imagine you are having a coffee chat with a friend, and you want to start talking about something that is an undeniable fact. You don't need to spend five minutes explaining basic physics! Instead, you might say: “大家都知道，地球是圆的。” (Dàjiā dōu zhīdào, dìqiú shì yuán de.) This translates roughly to: "As everyone knows, the Earth is round." The phrase 众所周知 (zhòng suǒ zhī) acts as a conversational shortcut. It's a discourse marker—which just means it’s a signal word that organizes your speech and tells the listener how you want them to interpret what follows.

Picture this: You are presenting a report, and instead of listing basic facts everyone already accepts (like "water is wet"), using 众所周知 helps you quickly establish common ground with your audience. It signals, "We both know this fact; let's move on to the exciting part!" It’s like tapping your finger lightly on the table to say, "Okay, focus here."

The simple rule is: Only use 众所周知 when the information you are about to share is truly general knowledge—something that 99% of people in the situation already accept as fact. If you use it for something debatable or specific to only one person, your listeners might feel patronized (like being talked down to).

Try it yourself: Think of a fundamental truth—perhaps gravity, or Monday coming after Sunday. Write a simple sentence using 众所周知 to introduce that obvious fact, making sure the tone is light and collaborative!
