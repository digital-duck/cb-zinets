🌸 Phrase 一路平安

Imagine you are saying goodbye to a friend who is departing on a long journey—maybe they are going back to their hometown or visiting another city. Instead of just saying "Bye," Chinese speakers often use 一路平安 (yī lù píng ān). This phrase is a heartfelt wish, meaning "safe travels" or "smooth journey."

Picture this phrase piece by piece: 一 simply means "one"; 路 refers to the road or path; and 平安 (píng ān) is a beautiful compound word that means peace and safety. When put together, 一路平安 literally wishes for one continuous road filled only with peace and safety. It is much more meaningful than just saying goodbye because it carries tangible good wishes for the person’s well-being throughout their trip.

The simple rule to remember is: Use 一路平安 whenever you are seeing someone off who is traveling or embarking on a significant journey. Since this phrase already contains the entire wish, you do not need to worry about grammar; just recall the whole unit! It acts as a perfect farewell blessing.

Try it yourself by imagining a scenario: Your family member is leaving for the airport. Instead of saying "Have a safe trip," practice saying 一路平安 to them. When they leave, remember that you are wishing them safety from start to finish!
