🍃 承

Imagine you are watching a baton relay race. The runners don't start from scratch; they must receive the baton from the person before them and then carry it to the next person. This act of passing and picking up—that is the essence of 承 (pronounced 'chéng'). It simply means "to continue," or "to receive and pass on."
