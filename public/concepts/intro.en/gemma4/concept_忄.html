🌱 忄

Have you ever felt a sudden, warm flutter in your chest when remembering someone or thinking about an exciting possibility? That quick burst of emotional anticipation—that feeling that makes butterflies take flight in your stomach—is what we mean by deep human emotion. These kinds of feelings are perfectly captured by the component $\text{忄}$! This character is known as the "heart radical," and its simple job is to signal that the word connected to it deals entirely with internal emotions or mental states.

Picture this: Ancient scribes didn't draw a literal heart, but they used this stylized shape ($\text{忄}$) because it points directly to our emotional center—the part of us where we process feelings and moods
