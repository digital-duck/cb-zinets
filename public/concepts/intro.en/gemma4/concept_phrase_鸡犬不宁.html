🌸 Phrase 鸡犬不宁

Imagine a busy market suddenly hears a rumor—a rumor that could cause panic or confusion throughout the community. Even if the initial news is minor, the resulting chaos causes people to rush, vendors to scream, and general disorder to spread rapidly through every corner. This widespread state of anxiety and commotion is what we describe using the phrase 鸡犬不宁 (jī quǎn bù níng). Literally meaning "chickens and dogs are restless," it describes a situation where nothing is calm; the turmoil spreads from one source to affect everything nearby.

Instead, its ripple effect causes widespread worry, nervousness, and disorder across many different areas of life. The initial shock quickly turns into collective restlessness, making the entire environment feel unsettled, like chickens running and dogs barking in panic.
