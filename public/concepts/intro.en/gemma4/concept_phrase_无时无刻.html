🌸 Phrase 无时无刻

Imagine you are describing a pet that is always begging for food. You wouldn't just say "The dog begs." Instead, you would add more detail: "The dog begs constantly." The phrase 无时无刻 acts exactly like this—it helps us put a time stamp on an action, showing that something happens continuously or frequently.
