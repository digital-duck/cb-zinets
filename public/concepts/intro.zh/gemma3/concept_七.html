Let’s start with a number you know – seven! You’ve probably counted on your fingers before, or maybe heard stories about lucky numbers. In Chinese, ‘七’ (qī) is a really important number. It can mean good luck, or sometimes bad luck, depending on how it's used.

Simple rule: ‘七’ (qī) means the number seven. It's like six plus one, or five minus one. We write it as $7$ in numbers.
