🌸 Phrase 后会有期

Let’s talk about a really common phrase in Chinese: 后会有期 (hòu yǒu qī). It's something you’ll hear a lot, especially when someone is saying goodbye. It literally translates to “after there will be” but what it means is much warmer than that.

Everyday Example: Imagine you’re chatting with a friend, and they say “后会有期!” before hanging up the phone. It doesn't mean "I'll contact you eventually, maybe never!" Instead, it’s like saying, “I hope we see each other again sometime.” It's a gentle way to end a conversation with an optimistic feeling.

Picture this: You’re leaving a party and your host says 后会有期 as you step out the door. They aren't promising a specific time for another meeting; they are just wishing you well and expressing hope for future contact.

Simple rule: 后会有期 is a polite way of saying goodbye that suggests you want to see someone again in the future, without making any firm promises about when that might happen. It's a friendly and hopeful farewell.

Try it yourself: The next time you say goodbye to someone, even just in your head, try saying “后会有期.” You’ll immediately feel like you're adding a little bit of warmth and optimism to the moment! It’s a really useful phrase to have in your vocabulary.
