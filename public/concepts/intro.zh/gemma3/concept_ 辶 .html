Everyday Example: Think about climbing a mountain. You start at the bottom and, step by step, you move upwards, right? That’s what 辶 visually embodies.

Picture This: Imagine you're drawing an arrow pointing upwards. Now, add a little curve to that arrow – just like this stroke: 辶. It still represents the upward direction, but now it also suggests the action of moving in that direction.

Simple Rule: The stroke 辶 is always associated with rising, ascending, or progressing from a lower position to a higher one. It’s never used to indicate going downwards or staying level. It's like a visual cue for "upward journey."
