🌸 Phrase 七嘴八舌

Let’s talk about something that happens all the time – when lots of people are talking at once! Imagine a crowded market, full of vendors shouting their wares and shoppers excitedly discussing prices. You hear bits and pieces of conversations blending together; it’s a bit chaotic, isn't it? That’s kind of what “七嘴八舌” describes.

Picture this: you’re at a family dinner, and everyone – your grandma, your cousins, your siblings - all want to share their opinions about the movie they just watched. Each person is saying something different, adding their own thoughts and feelings. It can feel like there's no single clear story being told!

Simply put, “七嘴八舌” (qī zuǐ bā shé) means a situation where many people are speaking at once, often with conflicting or overlapping ideas. The literal translation – "seven mouths, eight tongues" – paints a vivid picture of a noisy, busy scene filled with multiple voices. It’s not necessarily about disagreement; it just describes the effect of having numerous people talking simultaneously.

Maybe you were brainstorming ideas with friends, or perhaps you were listening to a lively debate at work.
