🍃 孛

Let’s talk about ‘孛’, which sounds a little mysterious at first. It's a really important idea when we're thinking about big dreams and planning for the future.

Everyday Example: Imagine you want to learn how to play the guitar. You might start with simple chords, like C, G, and D. You don’t try to master all 24 chords immediately! You build up your skills gradually, one chord at a time.

Picture this: Now imagine a family with lots of children. They have many hopes and wishes for each child – maybe one wants to be a doctor, another an artist, and another a computer programmer. Each of these individual aspirations is like a ‘孛’.

Simple rule: '孛’ (bō) basically means “a starting point” or “a building block.” It represents something small that you can take and grow into something bigger. It’s the initial step in achieving a larger goal. Think of it as laying the first brick for a huge castle – you need that one brick before you can build anything else!

Try it yourself: Let's say your big dream is to write a novel. Your ‘孛’ might be writing just one page a day. That one page is your starting point, and by building on it consistently, you create the whole story. It’s all about taking small steps!
