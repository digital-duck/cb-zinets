Simple Rule: “也” is used to indicate that something similar or related is happening in the same context as something else. It’s a way of saying, "Me too!" or "Just like me!". Importantly, it often emphasizes a connection between two actions or situations.

Try it Yourself: Let's construct a simple sentence. Suppose you are enjoying a cup of tea. You could say, “我喜欢喝茶，你也喜欢喝茶。” (Wǒ xǐhuan hē chá, nǐ yě xǐhuan hē chá – I like to drink tea, you also like to drink tea). Notice how "也" links your enjoyment of tea with a potential shared interest.
