🌸 Phrase 争分夺秒

Let’s talk about a really useful phrase in Chinese: 争分夺秒 (zhēng fēn duò miǎo). It sounds complicated, but it actually describes something we all do – making the most of every moment!

Everyday example: Imagine you're waiting for your favorite TV show to start. You could spend that time scrolling through social media, or you could read a book. The choice is yours, and how you use those minutes matters.

Picture this: You’re on a long train journey. There are only a few hours left until you arrive at your destination. Do you spend the whole time staring out the window, or do you try to catch up on work, read, or even just relax?

Simple rule: 争分夺秒 literally means “divide and seize seconds.” It’s about recognizing that every second is valuable and trying to use it wisely. “分” (fēn) means “minute” or "part," and “秒” (miǎo) means “to seize” or "grab."

Try it yourself: Think about a time you felt like time was slipping away – maybe you were stuck in traffic, or waiting for an appointment. How could you have used that time differently to make the experience better? Could you have listened to a podcast, practiced mindfulness, or simply planned your day more effectively? The key is to be aware and proactive!
