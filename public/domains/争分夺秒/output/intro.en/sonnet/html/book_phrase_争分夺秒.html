🌸 Phrase 争分夺秒

Imagine you are in a cooking competition and the timer is ticking down — every single second counts. You can't afford to waste even a moment chopping slowly or searching for a spoon. You race, you hustle, you make every second work for you. That feeling is exactly what 争分夺秒 (zhēng fēn duó miǎo) captures.

Picture this: The phrase is built from four characters. 争 means "to compete" or "to fight for." 分 means "minute." 夺 means "to seize" or "to grab." 秒 means "second." Put them together and you get: fighting for every minute, seizing every second. The image is vivid — time itself becomes something precious that you must grab with both hands.

Simple rule: Use 争分夺秒 to describe a situation where time is extremely urgent and every moment must be used wisely. It often appears in emergencies, competitions, or high-pressure work.

救援队员争分夺秒地赶往现场。 The rescue team raced against every minute and second to reach the scene.
