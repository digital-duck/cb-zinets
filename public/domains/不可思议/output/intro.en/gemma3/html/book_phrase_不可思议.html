Simple Rule: “不可思议” is used when something seems surprising or difficult to believe. It highlights the feeling of amazement or astonishment. Essentially, it describes something exceeding your normal expectations.

You'd likely exclaim, “这太不可思议了！” (Zhè tài bù kě sī yì le!) – "This is incredible!” The key here is that something has happened that feels beyond what is normally expected or possible. It’s about expressing your genuine reaction to something extraordinary.
