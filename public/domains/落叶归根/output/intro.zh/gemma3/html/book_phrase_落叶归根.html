Simple rule: “落叶归根” means that things that seem to leave or disappear often return to where they started. It’s like the leaves returning to the roots of the tree – the place from which they came. The root is the source, the beginning.

This concept emphasizes cycles and interconnectedness – recognizing that everything is part of a larger whole and will eventually find its way back to its origin.
