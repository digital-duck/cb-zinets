Picture this: Imagine you are playing tug-of-war. The simple rule here is: An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion, unless acted upon by a Net Force ($\sum \vec{F}$). If you want to change something’s speed or direction, you must apply a force! For example, if you hit a baseball (applying a large force), its velocity changes instantly.
