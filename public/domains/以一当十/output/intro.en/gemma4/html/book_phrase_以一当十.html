🌸 Phrase 以一当十

Imagine you are baking a cake, but instead of needing ten different ingredients, you discover one incredibly versatile ingredient—say, cocoa powder—that can replicate the depth of chocolate, the richness of coffee, and the aroma of vanilla all at once. This concept is called $\text{以一当十}$ (yǐ yī dàng shí), which literally means "to treat one as ten." In simple terms, it describes finding a single resource, technique, or idea that can achieve a disproportionately large amount of impact or value across many different areas. It's about maximizing output while minimizing the unique inputs required.

Picture this: Instead of hiring ten expensive specialists for ten different tasks (Task A requires Specialist 1, Task B requires Specialist 2, etc.), you invest in one highly skilled project manager and a standardized operating system. This single point of leverage—the OS or the person—suddenly provides expertise across all ten areas, solving complex problems that previously required massive resources.

The simple rule for $\text{以一当十}$ is: Identify your single most powerful resource (your 'one') and figure out how to apply it systematically and adaptively across multiple domains. When you see a need requiring $N$ separate solutions, ask yourself if one core concept or tool can serve as the foundation for all of them.

Try it yourself! Think about a complicated hobby you enjoy, like gardening. Instead of buying ten specialized tools (a specific trowel, a dedicated weeder, etc.), what is the single most useful, foundational tool that significantly improves your efficiency across all garden tasks?
