It is an idiomatic phrase used as a heartfelt promise, meaning "we won't be separated," or more poetically, "our bond will remain strong."

The simple rule is this: Use 不见散 when you are making a profound promise to someone dear—a friend, partner, or family member—that your relationship will endure temporary separation.
