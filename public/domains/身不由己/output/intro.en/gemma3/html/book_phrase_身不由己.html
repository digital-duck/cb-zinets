The phrase “身不由己” (shēn bù yóu jǐ) means "circumstances beyond one’s control." Let's break that down:

  • 身 (shēn): This refers to you, your situation, or your circumstances – basically, what’s happening around you.
  • 不由 (bù yóu): This means “not under” or “not in your power.” It suggests a lack of control.
  • 己 (jǐ): This simply translates to "yourself" or "one's own."

So, putting it together, “身不由己” describes a situation where something happens that you can’t influence, regardless of how much you might want to change it. It acknowledges the power of external forces and circumstances.
