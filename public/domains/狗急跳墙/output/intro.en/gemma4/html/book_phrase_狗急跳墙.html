Where do we use this tool? Everywhere! When discussing political maneuvering, gǒu jí tiào qiáng helps us understand forced radical changes in policy (the application of Political Science). Even in everyday disagreements, if someone escalates needlessly out of panic, we can point to the phrase to explain their underlying emotional state.

The beauty of this concept is its universality: it moves us from merely describing what happened (e.g., "He yelled at everyone") to explaining why and under what pressure it happened ("He acted in a $\text{gǒu jí tiào qiáng}$ manner"). This framework gives you immediate insight into the psychology of crisis.
