Everyday Example: Imagine your friend wants to learn how to play the guitar. They might say they have 雄心勃 – meaning they aren’t just casually interested; they genuinely want to dedicate themselves to learning, practice regularly, and eventually become proficient!

That drive, that intense desire to achieve something great, is very much captured by 雄心勃. Simple Rule: The phrase suggests a combination of two important things: “雄 (xióng)” means “brave” or “strong,” representing courage and power, and “心 (xīn)” means "heart" or "mind," symbolizing intention and drive. “勃 (bó)” is an auxiliary character indicating vigorous growth or expansion – think of a seedling pushing through the soil!
